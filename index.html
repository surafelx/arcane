Digital Financial Services

As an authorized CBE Birr Super Agent and certified Safaricom Ethiopia partner, we provide end-to-end digital financial services including eKYC registration, agent network development, merchant distribution, and platform integration across Addis Ababa and regional Ethiopia.

About Us

A Technology Partner Built for Ethiopia's Growing Markets
