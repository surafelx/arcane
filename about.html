About Arcane Technologies PLC

Registered in Ethiopia since 2021, Arcane Technologies is a digital financial services specialist, authorized brand distributor, and technology solutions provider.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower businesses across Ethiopia with innovative technology solutions and reliable distribution services. We bridge the gap between enterprises and their customers through digital financial services, telecom products, and essential consumer goods — delivering value that drives growth and operational excellence for our partners.

🔭

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's most trusted partner in digital financial services and distribution — known for integrity, reliability, and the ability to connect brands with markets across every region of the country.

Our Story

Built for Ethiopia's Growing Economy

Arcane Technologies PLC was founded in 2021 in Addis Ababa with a clear understanding of Ethiopia's market potential — and the gaps that needed filling.

From the start, we focused on three areas: digital financial inclusion (connecting underserved populations with CBE Birr and mobile money services), reliable distribution (bringing quality consumer brands to market), and technology infrastructure (lighting, security, and enterprise software).

Today, we operate across Addis Ababa, Oromia, and Southern Ethiopia, serving as an authorized CBE Birr Super Agent, a certified Safaricom Ethiopia partner, and a trusted distributor for brands including Dachi, Lilac Ethiopia, and Elleni.

We've participated in major rollouts including the Safaricom Ethiopia eKYC project — supporting 78 branches and 1,170+ registrations in just three months — proving our ability to operate at scale.
